MAT Programmation Comunity


A bilingual Discord server (FR-EN) open to all computer enthusiasts, regardless of their level or preferred field. Here, you'll find an active and diverse community, ready to help each other, exchange ideas, collaborate on projects, or simply chat about tech. The atmosphere can be customized to your preferences using the server settings, and you can even suggest ideas to improve any aspect of the community: each suggestion is subject to a collective vote. The associated YouTube channel is open to all members wishing to share content, subject to the rules. Whether you're here to learn, share, or create, this server is for you!

‎

MAT Programmation Community was created on January 5, 2025 by the MAT Services as a Discord server for programming assistance. It then grew and now gives an open community to all, centered on computers, actually, she got a website, a YouTube Channel and a WIKI.
